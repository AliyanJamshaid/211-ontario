Description
Assessment and treatment for those who have been recently sexually assaulted, those who have been recently assaulted by a partner or children who have been abused

Chantel's Place
  • one-to-one care in a safe, private, secure unit
  • assessment, documentation of injuries
  • treatment
  • safety planning, emotional support
  • information concerning medical, legal and counseling options
  • follow-up medical care including re-documentation of injuries, range of medical services and referral to social service agencies
Counselling
  • free counselling for people who have been referred through the medical services
  • counselling at Mississauga Hospital site
Eligibility
Persons 12 years or older who have been recently sexually assaulted (usually within the past 12 days); persons 16 years or older who have recent injuries as a result of domestic violence; children under 12 who require care, assessment and or documentation as a result of sexual assault/abuse
Application Process
Admission through the Mississauga Hospital Emergency Department
Documents Required
Bring Ontario Health Card or other health insurance information if possible
